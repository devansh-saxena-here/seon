# 📖 Project Overview

> **SEON (Smart Energy Optimization Network)** is an AI-powered Smart Energy Management Prototype developed as part of the **1M1B Green Skills & Applied AI Internship**, supported by **Microsoft**.
>
> The project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can help organizations monitor electricity usage, understand consumption patterns, receive intelligent recommendations, and encourage sustainable energy practices through an interactive web platform.
>
> Rather than relying on complex hardware infrastructure, SEON focuses on presenting a realistic digital prototype that showcases how AI, modern dashboards, and sustainability insights can work together to improve energy awareness and decision-making.
>
> The platform allows users to visualize energy consumption through interactive charts, monitor buildings and devices using realistic demo data, communicate with an AI assistant powered by Google Gemini, explore ESG-focused sustainability insights, and generate energy reports within a clean and modern user experience.
>
> SEON has been designed as a proof-of-concept (MVP Prototype) to demonstrate the future potential of intelligent energy management systems. The project combines AI-powered recommendations with an intuitive interface to showcase how organizations can reduce unnecessary energy consumption, improve operational efficiency, and promote environmentally responsible practices.
>
> While the current version uses realistic simulated data, the architecture has been designed in a way that future versions can integrate with smart meters, IoT devices, cloud databases, and real-time monitoring systems.

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Many organizations still depend on manual electricity monitoring or monthly electricity bills to understand their energy consumption. This approach makes it difficult to identify unnecessary energy usage, detect abnormal consumption patterns, or take timely corrective actions.

As a result, organizations often experience:

- High electricity consumption
- Increased operational costs
- Lack of energy visibility
- Limited sustainability awareness
- Inefficient resource utilization
- Difficulty in monitoring overall energy performance

SEON addresses this challenge by demonstrating how an AI-powered platform can simplify energy monitoring and provide intelligent recommendations through an easy-to-use web interface.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

SEON provides a centralized web platform where users can:

- Monitor electricity usage using interactive dashboards
- Analyze energy consumption trends
- View AI-generated energy-saving recommendations
- Explore sustainability and ESG insights
- Generate visual energy reports
- Interact with an AI Assistant for energy-related queries

The platform uses realistic demo data to simulate how an intelligent Smart Energy Management System could function in real-world organizations.
